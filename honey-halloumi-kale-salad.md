# Honey Halloumi Kale Salad

## Ingredients

**Salad**

- 2 bunches kale, chopped
- ⅓ cup dry quinoa (about 1 cup cooked)
- 1 can (396ml) lentils, drained and rinsed
- 1 pint yellow sungold tomatoes, halved
- 1 shallot, diced
- ½ cup almonds, toasted and chopped
- chili flakes, for serving
- 1 lemon wedge, for serving

**Creamy Greek Dressing**

- juice of 1 lemon
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons plain Greek yogurt
- 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, minced
- 2 tablespoons fresh mint, minced
- 2 tablespoons fresh basil, minced
- 3 garlic cloves, pressed
- 1½ teaspoons salt
- ½ teaspoon pepper

**Halloumi**

- 450-600g halloumi, cut into ½-inch slices
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 tablespoons liquid honey
- 1 lemon wedge

## Instructions

1. Massage chopped kale with a drizzle of olive oil and a pinch of salt for 1-2 minutes. Set aside.
1. Cook quinoa according to package directions.
1. Whisk together all dressing ingredients.
1. Combine kale, cooked quinoa, and lentils. Drizzle with dressing and toss to coat.
1. Toast almonds in a dry pan over medium heat for 5-6 minutes until golden and fragrant.
1. Pat halloumi slices dry with paper towels. In the same pan with olive oil, cook halloumi 3-5 minutes per side, drizzling each side with 1 tablespoon of honey, until golden brown. Squeeze lemon over the cheese.
1. Top salad with halloumi, tomatoes, shallots, toasted almonds, chili flakes, and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.

## Notes

- Keep dressing separate from greens until serving to prevent sogginess.
- Cook halloumi fresh just before serving for best texture.
- Use fresh herbs only — dried won't give the same flavor.
- Quinoa can be swapped for couscous; kale for spinach; Greek yogurt for sour cream.
